Help

This tool is straightforward to use, but here are some tips and answers to common questions:

  • Typing/Pasting Text: Enter or paste your text into the large text box on the main page.
  • Live Counts: Word, paragraph, and character counts update instantly as you type.
  • Paragraphs: Paragraph count is based on text separated by new lines.
  • Clear & Copy: Use the "Clear" button to erase all text, or "Copy" to copy your text to the clipboard.
  • Writing Goals: Set and track your daily word count goals on the Writing Goals page. Your progress is saved locally.
  • Privacy: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to a server.
  • Supported Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, and most modern browsers are supported.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does this tool work offline? Yes, once loaded, you can use it without an internet connection.
  • Can I count words in other languages? Yes, it works with any language that uses spaces or line breaks to separate words and paragraphs.
  • Is my text safe? Absolutely! All text stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
