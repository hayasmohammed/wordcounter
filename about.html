About Word Counter

Word Counter is your modern, privacy-first writing companion for students, professionals, bloggers, and anyone working with words. Our mission is to provide a distraction-free, all-in-one toolkit for text analysis—right in your browser, with no sign-up and no data tracking.

  • Live Word, Character & Paragraph Counts: Instantly see your stats update as you type or paste text. Perfect for essays, assignments, blog posts, and more.
  • Writing Goals Tracker: Set a daily word goal and watch your progress update in real time. Your goals and writing are saved only on your device.
  • Text Statistics Tool: Get detailed stats: sentence and paragraph count, average word length, longest and shortest words, and your most common word.
  • Permanent Dark Mode: Enjoy a sleek, eye-friendly interface that's easy to use day or night—no light mode distractions.
  • Privacy Guaranteed: Your text never leaves your browser. There are no ads, no tracking, and no server-side storage—ever.
  • Mobile-Friendly & Fast: Works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops. No installation required.

Word Counter is built for everyone who needs quick, accurate text analysis and writing tools, without the clutter or privacy worries. Whether you’re a student keeping to a word limit, a blogger checking your writing stats, or a novelist tracking your daily output, you’ll find the tools you need here.

Have ideas or found a bug? We love feedback! Email us at help@wordcounter.example.com and help us make Word Counter even better.
